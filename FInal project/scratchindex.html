Surfing Basics

Surfing is a water sport where the surfer rides waves on a surfboard. Here are some basic things you need to know:

  1. Choosing the right board
    • Longboards are good for beginners.
    • Shortboards are great for more experienced surfers.
  2. Understanding waves
    • Waves are formed by wind and tides.
    • Different types of waves include point breaks, reef breaks, and beach breaks.
  3. Surfing Etiquette
    • Don't drop in on someone's wave.
    • Respect the local surfing community.
